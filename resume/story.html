Ship Life

Jan 2001 - Nov 2001

Following high school and a few college courses I wanted to get out and see the world. I joined the twelve member crew of the White Holly and was tasked with bringing a ship from Virginia to the Marshall Islands. It was going to be a medical christian missionary ship for islanders. The journey taught me leadership, discipline and dedication.

California Baptist University

2002 - 2006

After a year of extreme discipline I was prepared to get a degree. Now with a new curiosity about religions, I enrolled in seminary. I felt ministry would be my calling. After serving in the ministry for a short while I decide the pulpit wasn’t where my interest and talent lay.

Domestic Service Projects

2003 - 2006

While I was in seminary I wanted to do something useful with my spring and winter breaks so I began organizing domestic service trips. I organized and lead multiple teams of students to different cities to do service for others. One such trip was helping residents rebuild their lives after Hurricane Katrina.
